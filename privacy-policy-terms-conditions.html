Privacy Policy and SMS Terms & Conditions

Privacy Policy

At Landmark Credit Services, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

What Information We Collect: We collect personal information provided by you, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, and current credit profile details.

How Information is Used: Your information is used strictly to provide professional credit advocacy services, including consultations, account updates, and appointment reminders.

Sharing of Information: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. SMS consent and phone numbers obtained for SMS purposes are not shared with third parties or affiliates.

SMS Terms & Conditions

1. SMS Consent Communication: The information (Phone Numbers) obtained as part of the SMS consent process will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Types of SMS Communications: By opting in, you consent to receive text messages from Landmark Credit Services related to the following:

  • Appointment reminders
  • Follow-up messages regarding your credit restoration
  • Billing inquiries and account notifications

3. Message Frequency: Message frequency may vary depending on the type of communication. You may receive up to 5 SMS messages per week related to your file.

4. Potential Fees: Standard message and data rates may apply, depending on your carrier’s pricing plan. These fees may vary if the message is sent domestically or internationally.

5. Opt-In Method: You may opt-in to receive SMS messages from Landmark Credit Services in the following ways:

  • Verbally, during a conversation with our advocates
  • By submitting an online consultation form on our website

6. Opt-Out Method: You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time. To do so, simply reply "STOP" to any SMS message you receive. Alternatively, you can contact us directly to request removal from our messaging list.

7. Help: If you are experiencing any issues, you can reply with the keyword HELP for assistance or contact us via our website at www.landmarkcredit.com.

8. Standard Disclosures:

  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt out at any time by texting "STOP."
  • Messaging frequency may vary.
